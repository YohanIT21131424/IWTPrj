WHO ARE WE?

We are an event photography and videography company specialising in Wedding photography and cinematography. We also cater for Corporate Events, festivals, birthdays, anniversary and portrait photography.

Our aim is to provide unique and creative photography and cinematic video that captures memories that will last a life time.

We are based at Hyde park avenue colombo but are available to travel anywhere in country. On special request we also travel overseas events.

We have affordable packages with a no interest payment option. All our packages can be customized to our clients needs.

We understand choosing your photographer for your wedding day is extremely important and so we offer a high quality and friendly service that will go the extra mile to make your special day memorable.
