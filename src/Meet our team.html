Meet Our Team

Our team is your team. When your mission is to be better, faster and smarter. Our team gets a natural high from continuously driving demand for our clients’ products and services (and looks darn good doing it). We are transparent, strategic, hard-hitting, and results-driven, with a major soft spot – we truly care about our clients.
So take a few minutes and dive in to learn a little bit about our talented team and all the great things we can do together.

I'm Cassie Liz, a event photographer.I launched Camera crew Photography in 2005 with a dream and a simple online listing for families looking to have their picture taken.

I love working with both men & women photographers who are kindred spirits and pioneers, creating something beautiful for themselves and the world.

I believe that your brand photos should be a complete reflection of the commitment, expertise and passion you bring to the table. I go deeper than just a perfectly composed picture—I capture the heart you pour into your business.I'll be cheering you on as you courageously step out to share your story.

Sayuru Edirisooriya

Professional Photographer

I am Sayuru Edirisooriya

Sayuru Edirisooriya has been a professional photographer for nearly 10 years, during which time he has worked on both commercial lifestyle shoots for big brands and editorial portrait and photojournalistic commissions for major publications. He is currently working on a long-term personal documentary project here.

He also hosts and produces the popular, fortnightly photography podcast, A Small Voice: Conversations With Photographers, on which he chats intimately with a wide array of well-known photographers about their work, life and practice. Sayuru has been designing and building websites for himself and numerous others – including fellow photographers – for many years and has been focussing exclusively on the Squarespace platform since 2010.

Gayan Abeyrathna
×

Blurred Background

I am Gayan Abeyrathna

Gayan Abeyrathna is a Still Life Photographer and Director, working across fine art, editorial and advertising platforms. He has a graphic and minimalist style that evokes a sense of luxury and simple beauty.

Frequently experimenting with narrative landscapes and the interaction between inanimate objects surrounding us in everyday life. Creating a visual language where there is a playful but intelligent balance between the odd and the familiar. Gayan graduated from Falmouth University in 2010, with first class honours, and since been working from London.

Sewmi Kariyawasam
×

Fashion Photographer

I am Sewmi Kariyawasam

Fashion and portrait photographer Sewmi Kariyawasam is an artist making head waves in the UK scene and abroad with her style that transcends the boundaries of classic fashion photography and passes into forms of the artistic expression. Her work was exhibited in New York, Paris, London, Helsinki or Sevastopol and has been published in various magazines and publications such as Vogue Italia.

More than a digital camera, Sewmi extensively uses analogue cameras such as large format camera Sinar and medium format Bronica.
